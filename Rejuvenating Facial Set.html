This set is specifically designed for acne-prone and oily skin to quickly remove dead skin cells. It helps smooth the face by eliminating pimple marks and dark spots. How to Use

  • Wash your face using the Kojic Acid Soap and pat the skin dry.
  • Apply the Rejuvenating Facial Toner using a cotton pad across the entire face.
