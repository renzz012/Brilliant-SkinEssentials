AHA Rejuvenating Serum

Ultimate Skin Booster & Whitening• 30mL

Product Description

This serum is formulated with Alpha Hydroxy Acids to smoothen skin texture and lighten spots. It acts as a powerful booster that can accelerate the results of other products. The serum helps exfoliate the top layer of skin to reveal a brighter layer. It is particularly effective for correcting discoloration on stubborn areas like elbows or knees. Regular use can significantly improve skin clarity and provide a uniform skin tone.

How to Use

  • Mix 2 drops with your body lotion for overall brightening.
  • Alternatively, apply directly to dark areas like knees or elbows.
  • Use this product only at night to avoid sun sensitivity.
  • Massage it in until the skin absorbs the serum completely.
  • Always wear sunscreen during the day while using this serum.
