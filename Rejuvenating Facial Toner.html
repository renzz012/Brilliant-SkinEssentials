Rejuvenating Facial Toner

Extra Strength Exfoliating Liquid • 60mL

PHP 220.00

Product Description

This extra-strength toner is designed to deep-clean pores and remove stubborn, deep-seated oil. It effectively removes dead skin cells to reveal a much clearer and smoother skin layer. This formula is highly recommended for acne-prone individuals to help clear existing skin blemishes. It prepares your face for better absorption of serums and nourishing night creams. Regular nighttime use can significantly improve skin texture and reduce the look of scars.

How to Use

  • Wash your face with soap and pat it completely dry.
  • Put a small amount of toner on a clean cotton ball.
  • Gently swipe the cotton across your face in an upward motion.
  • Avoid the sensitive areas around the eyes and the mouth.
  • Allow it to dry before applying your nighttime facial cream.
