Product Description

This premium serum is designed to provide an instant "glass skin" effect for everyone. It works to tighten the skin and visibly minimize the appearance of enlarged pores. Adding this to your routine helps achieve a firmer and more polished complexion.

How to Use

  • Cleanse and tone your face using your preferred products.
  • Apply 2 to 3 drops of the serum onto your face.
  • Gently pat the serum into your skin using your fingertips.
