Sunscreen Gel-Cream

UVA+UVB Protection SPF 30 • 50g

PHP 180.00

Product Description

This gel-cream offers essential broad-spectrum protection against the sun’s harmful ultraviolet rays. It acts as a primary shield to prevent sun damage and the formation of dark spots. The specialized formula leaves a non-sticky matte finish that feels lightweight on the skin. It also functions perfectly as a primer, creating a smooth base for your daily makeup. Protecting your skin from UV exposure is the most vital step in any whitening routine.

How to Use

  • Cleanse your face and apply your usual morning toner.
  • Squeeze a generous amount of sunscreen onto your fingertips..
  • Apply evenly to your face and neck 30 minutes before going out.
  • Re-apply every 2 hours if you are staying under the sun.
  • Use this as the final step of your daytime skincare regimen.
