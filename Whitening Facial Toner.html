Product Description

This mild toner is perfect for maintaining a bright complexion after a rejuvenation cycle. It helps refine the appearance of pores while providing a gentle brightening effect. The formula is safe for long-term use without causing any visible peeling or redness. It keeps the skin clean and radiant by removing any impurities left after washing. This is the best choice for those who want a lasting glow without intensity.

How to Use

  • Cleanse your face with a mild soap or facial wash.
  • Dampen a soft cotton pad with the whitening toner liquid.
  • Swipe the pad gently over your entire face and neck.
  • Focus on the T-zone area to help refine larger pores
  • Use every morning and night to maintain your white complexion..
