Tomato Micro-Exfoliating Soap

Natural Skin Vitalizing Bar • 135g

PHP 115.00

Product Description

This gentle soap is specially crafted for those with sensitive skin or teenagers. It is enriched with natural tomato extract to provide a healthy pinkish glow. The mild formulation cleanses the skin deeply without causing any unwanted irritation or dryness. It helps to vitalize the skin's appearance by gently removing dead skin cells daily. This is the ideal choice for maintaining a fresh and youthful complexion naturally.

How to Use

  • Wet your face with water to prepare for cleansing.
  • Create a gentle lather by rubbing the soap in your hands.
  • Apply the lather to your face using very light pressure.
  • Rinse immediately with water to prevent over-cleansing sensitive skin.
  • Gently pat your face dry and proceed with your skincare.
