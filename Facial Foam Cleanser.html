Facial Foam Cleanser

Whitening & Deep Cleansing Foam • 100mL

PHP 350.00

Product Description

This innovative cleanser produces a rich foam that lifts away excess oil and dirt. It features a built-in silicone brush head that helps to unclog pores manually and effectively. The whitening formula helps brighten the skin while leaving it feeling incredibly fresh and clean. It is designed to be gentle for daily use while providing a professional-level clean. Your skin will look more luminous and feel smoother after every single wash.

How to Use

  • Wet your face with water to soften the skin surface.
  • Pump the bottle to release foam onto the silicone brush.
  • Gently massage the brush in circular motions around your face.
  • Focus on oily areas like the nose and the forehead.
  • Rinse thoroughly with cold water and clean the brush head.
