Micellar Deep Cleansing Water

Alcohol-Free Makeup Remover• 100mL

Product Description

This alcohol-free micellar water is a gentle way to remove makeup and environmental dirt. It is formulated to cleanse deeply without causing any dryness or skin stinging. The product is safe for the entire face, including the sensitive eyes and lips. It uses micelle technology to lift away impurities like a magnet without harsh rubbing. This is an essential item for keeping your skin clear after a long day.

How to Use

  • Soak a clean cotton pad with the micellar water liquid.
  • Gently wipe the pad across your face to remove dirt.
  • Hold the pad over closed eyes for seconds for makeup.
  • Wipe away until the cotton pad comes out completely clean.
  • No rinsing is required, but you may follow with washing.
