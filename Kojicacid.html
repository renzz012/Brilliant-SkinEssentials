Anti-Hyperpigmentation Bar • 135g

Product Description

This professional-grade soap is formulated to lighten skin tone and effectively fade stubborn dark spots. It works by gently exfoliating the skin's surface to reveal a much brighter complexion underneath. The active ingredients help dry out existing pimples quickly while preventing new ones from forming. Regular use results in a more radiant and glowing appearance for the entire body. It is the perfect solution for anyone struggling with uneven skin tone or minor blemishes.

How to Use

  • Wet the soap with water until it produces a rich lather.
  • Apply the foam to your face and body using gentle circular motions..
  • Allow the lather to stay on your skin for 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Rinse thoroughly with water until all residue is completely gone.
  • Pat your skin dry with a clean, soft towel.
